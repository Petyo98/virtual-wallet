About Us

Virtual Wallet is the easy and safe way to make funds transactions online.

Our services allow almost anyone to make online transaction without sharing their full financial information with anyone.

We have been in the market for online transactions for almost 10 years and we are still growing. At the start of 2018 our users have reached 140 million and we are expecting to be at 200 million by the end of 2020. Our services are currently available in 160 countries and we are hoping to soon be fully global with access everywhere. We support all currencies with no additional conversion fees. So far we have received 7 awards for excellence in our industry and are hoping to become the top provider of online transaction services by 2025.
